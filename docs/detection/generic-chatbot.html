Frequently Asked Questions

How do I return or exchange a product?
You can initiate a return or exchange within 30 days of purchase through your account dashboard. Go to Order History, select the order, and click "Request Return." You'll receive a prepaid shipping label via email.
What is covered under the warranty?
All Prism products come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. This does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications. Extended warranty plans are available at checkout.
How do I update firmware on my device?
Download the Prism Connect app on your phone or computer. Connect your device via USB or Bluetooth, and the app will automatically check for available updates. Follow the on-screen instructions to install.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. You can check availability and estimated delivery at checkout.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Log in to your account, go to Settings > Subscription, and click "Cancel Plan." Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months.
